Data Retention Schedule 2026

Version 2026.3 | Owner: Records and Privacy

SYNTHETIC TRAINING DOCUMENT - NOT AN ACTUAL COMPANY POLICY

Page 1 of 4

People records

  1. A former employee's personnel file is retained for 7 years after termination of employment.
  2. Records for an unsuccessful job applicant are retained for 2 years after the hiring decision.

Questions about this section should be directed to Records and Privacy.

Page 2 of 4

Finance records

  1. Supplier invoices and payment support are retained for 7 fiscal years after the fiscal year of payment.
  2. Filed corporate tax returns and supporting workpapers are retained for 10 years after filing.

Questions about this section should be directed to Records and Privacy.

Page 3 of 4

Customer service records

  1. A customer support ticket is retained for 3 years after the ticket is closed.
  2. A recorded customer support call is retained for 18 months after the recording date.

Questions about this section should be directed to Records and Privacy.

Page 4 of 4

Deletion and legal holds

  1. The automated retention purge runs once each quarter for records whose retention period has expired.
  2. A legal hold suspends scheduled deletion until the Legal Department issues a written release of the hold.

Questions about this section should be directed to Records and Privacy.
