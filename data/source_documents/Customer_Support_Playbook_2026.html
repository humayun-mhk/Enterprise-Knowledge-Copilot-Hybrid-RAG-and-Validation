Customer Support Playbook 2026

Version 2026.6 | Owner: Customer Experience

SYNTHETIC TRAINING DOCUMENT - NOT AN ACTUAL COMPANY POLICY

Page 1 of 4

Response targets

  1. A Priority 1 customer case has a 15-minute initial response target, available 24 hours a day and 7 days a week.
  2. A Priority 2 customer case has an initial response target of 2 business hours.

Questions about this section should be directed to Customer Experience.

Page 2 of 4

Escalation

  1. A Priority 1 case must be escalated to the support manager if it remains unresolved 30 minutes after opening.
  2. A support case involving suspected privacy or security impact must be escalated immediately to the Trust Response team.

Questions about this section should be directed to Customer Experience.

Page 3 of 4

Customer communication

  1. The assigned case owner must update the customer at least every 60 minutes while a Priority 1 case remains active.
  2. A written resolution summary must be sent to the customer within 2 business days after case resolution.

Questions about this section should be directed to Customer Experience.

Page 4 of 4

Credits and case records

  1. A support agent may approve a goodwill credit up to USD 100; a support manager may approve up to USD 500.
  2. A substantive customer interaction must be documented in the case record by the end of the same business day.

Questions about this section should be directed to Customer Experience.
